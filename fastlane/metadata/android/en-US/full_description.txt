ReShare converts documents, URLs, and images between formats directly on your device. Share a file, link, or image to ReShare from any app, pick your target format, and reshare the converted result -- all without leaving your phone.

Powered by Pandoc, ReShare supports a wide range of document formats:

INPUT FORMATS
- URLs (web pages, tweets, Instagram posts)
- Images (PNG, JPEG, WebP, GIF) -- converted to PDF
- Microsoft Word (.docx)
- PDF
- HTML
- EPUB
- LaTeX
- Markdown
- Org mode
- And more

OUTPUT FORMATS
- PDF
- Microsoft Word (.docx)
- HTML
- Markdown
- Plain text
- LaTeX
- And more

KEY FEATURES
- URL fetching: share a link and ReShare fetches and converts the content
- Image to PDF: share photos and get clean PDF output
- Local conversion: document processing happens on your device using Pandoc
- No data collection: no analytics, no tracking
- Share integration: receive files from any app via Android's share menu
- Conversion history: browse and re-share past conversions
- Template support: apply custom templates to your conversions
- Text preview: inspect converted text output before sharing

HOW IT WORKS
1. Share a document, URL, or image to ReShare from any app
2. Choose your desired output format
3. ReShare converts the content
4. Share the result to any app, or save it to your device

ReShare is free and open source.
